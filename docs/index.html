Pipeline

  1. Get your thoughts down
  2. Submit
  3. Forget
  4. Come back to an organized system

What it is

A small proof of concept (ongoing project) for turning raw notes into structured entries using on-device models when available, with a local heuristic fallback.

What it does

  • Capture fast: type or dictate.
  • Submit and enhance in the background.
  • Organize into simple properties (kind/status/area/priority/project).
  • Export your notes as JSON.
